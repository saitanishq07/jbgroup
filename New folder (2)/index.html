OUR TEAM

Mr. Jayvant Choudhary

As the visionary founder and chairman of JB Group, Mr. Jayvant Choudhary has built a diversified conglomerate that stands as a testament to entrepreneurial excellence and customer-first values. With over two decades of experience in the hospitality, events, and real estate sectors, he has transformed JB Group into one of Mumbai's most trusted names in premium services.

Under his leadership, JB Group has expanded from a single venture into a multi-faceted organization encompassing event management, luxury banquets, holiday resorts, catering services, real estate consultancy, and agricultural ventures. His commitment to quality and innovation has earned the group recognition across Maharashtra and beyond.

Mr. Choudhary's philosophy is simple yet powerful: "A satisfied customer is the best business strategy of all." This principle guides every decision and drives the group's relentless pursuit of excellence. His hands-on approach and attention to detail ensure that whether it's a grand wedding, a corporate event, or a property investment, clients receive nothing but the finest service.

Beyond business, Mr. Choudhary is deeply committed to social responsibility and community development, believing that true success is measured not just in profit, but in positive impact on society.
