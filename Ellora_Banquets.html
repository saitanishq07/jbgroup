About Ellora Banquets

Ellora Banquets is a premier venue located in the heart of Sanpada, Navi Mumbai. With state-of-the-art facilities and elegant interiors, we provide the perfect setting for your special occasions, whether it's a wedding, corporate event, or social gathering.

Our spacious halls can accommodate events of all sizes, from intimate gatherings to grand celebrations. With world-class amenities, professional service staff, and customizable packages, we ensure every event is executed flawlessly.

Located at Ellora Halls, Sector 11, Sanpada, 400705, we pride ourselves on creating memorable experiences that exceed expectations. Our dedicated team works closely with you to bring your vision to life.
